\section{Command Exit Codes}

Table~\ref{tab:exit-codes} lists all exit codes returned in response to control commands.  Per-command exit codes are documented alongside each command in Section~\ref{sec:commands}.

\begin{longtable}{cp{10cm}}
    \caption{Command Exit Codes} \label{tab:exit-codes} \\
    \toprule
    \textbf{Code} & \textbf{Description} \\

    \endfirsthead

    \toprule
    \textbf{Code} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \midrule
    \endhead

    \bottomrule
    \endfoot

    \hex{00} & Process accepted without error \\
    \hex{04} & Board programming failed \\
    \hex{05} & Board configuration failed \\
    \hex{07} & Invalid command arguments (unknown mode) \\
    \hex{08} & Board-level shutdown failed \\
    \hex{09} & Server startup failed \\
    \hex{0A} & Server shutdown failed \\
    \hex{0B} & Pipeline startup failed \\
    \hex{0C} & Blocking operation in progress (INI or SHT already running) \\
    \hex{0D} & Internal F-engine error condition \\
    \hex{0E} & Pipeline processing error \\
    \hex{0F} & Other internal error (e.g., timing monitor problem) \\
    \hex{63} & Subsystem not ready (in SHUTDWN or BOOTING state) \\
    \hex{FF} & Unknown command or unimplemented feature \\

\end{longtable}
